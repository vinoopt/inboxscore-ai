// Features

Everything you need to land in the inbox.

Built on Mailercloud's deliverability infrastructure. Each check is grounded in RFCs and real-world ISP behavior.

🔐

Authentication checks

SPF, DKIM, DMARC alignment with full nuance — pct, sp, fo, strict mode. We probe 47 DKIM selectors automatically.

🛡️

Blacklist monitor

Check 40+ DNSBLs for your sending IPs and your domain. We expand SPF includes to find every IP authorized to send.

🌐

IP reputation

Track your sending IPs across whitelists, reputation feeds, and ISP-specific signals. Catch issues before they affect delivery.

📊

Continuous monitoring

Daily scans, score history, and alerts when something breaks. Google Postmaster integration for inbox placement data.

🔒

TLS & MTA-STS

Verify in-transit encryption, certificate validity, and modern infrastructure signals. MTA-STS, TLS-RPT, BIMI, DNSSEC.

🤖

Actionable fixes

Every flag includes copy-paste DNS records, difficulty estimate, and time-to-fix. Top 3 actions prioritized by score impact.
